About Us

History

Chronoshift Expeditions first started when Dr. Juan Perez had a desire to hunt extinct monsters. He attended college and earned his engineering and theoretical physics PhD’s. Eventually, he developed a time machine to travel back in time to hunt the monsters that once ruled the planet. The time machine took roughly 12 years to develop, though the building of the machine didn’t take long. The part that took the longest was doing the math to figure out how to travel in time.

About the Machine

The machine was designed mainly by Dr. Juan Perez, the founder of Chronoshift Expeditions, Engineer, and Theoretical Physicist; the machine is designed to send people back in time in order to allow them to hunt the monsters that once ruled. The machine uses quantum mechanics to send people back in time which means that participants must be careful not to alter the future. time.
