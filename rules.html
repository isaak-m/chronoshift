Rules

Risks

There are many risks in time traveling, most prominently the risk of altering the future; the risk of altering the future is something that is always taken into consideration, that is why there are rules in place to prevent people from altering the future. Other risks include safety, where a participant is not guaranteed to come back, that is why there are rules in place to keep participants safe and to keep the guides safe as well.

Rules

At Chronoshift Expeditions, altering the future and safety are on top of the priority; because these are on top of the priority, rules must be set in place, which include:

  • Do NOT step off the path.
  • Listen and follow the instructions given by the guides.
  • Never bring anything back.
  • Never leave anything behind.

Safety

Safety concerns are always on the top of the priority, that is why there are rules set into place to ensure the participants and guides safety. These rules include

  • Listen and follow the instructions given by the guides
  • Do NOT step off the path
These rules are designed to ensure the participants safety and in order to ensure the safety of the future.

COVID-19 Protocols

Due to the world wide COVID-19 pandemic, Chronoshift Expeditions is taking into precaution the spreading of the virus. The safety of participants is a top priority here at Chronoshift Expeditions, which is why all participants must be vaccinated, tested, and be wearing a mask at all times.

Waiver

Participants must be wearing a mask at all times, vaccinated, and tested for COVID-19. All participants are to remain on the path and must obey what the guides ask of them. If a participant is to be injured during their journey, Chronoshift Expeditions is not at fault nor can they be sued for any injury that a participant may experience. If a participant is to be injured during their journey, then protocols will take place to bring said participant back to their time and bring them the proper medical attention.
